Our Community

The UC CCS community is made up of passionate students, dedicated faculty, and hardworking working students who keep the labs running every single day.

Students

Hundreds of BS Information Technology and BS Computer Science use the CCS labs daily — for coursework, projects, research, and free time practice. The sit-in monitoring system exists to serve them fairly and efficiently.

Working Students

Working students are the backbone of lab operations. They assist with sit-in logging, guide newcomers through the process, monitor lab occupancy, and ensure that every student who walks in gets the help they need.

Faculty and Staff

CCS faculty and laboratory staff oversee the system, maintain lab equipment, and ensure that policies are followed. Their oversight keeps the environment productive and welcoming for everyone.
