Rules & Regulations
University of Cebu Laboratory Rules and Regulations

To avoid penalties, please observe the following at all times:

  1. Students must present their ID before using the laboratory.
  2. No food, drinks, or smoking inside the laboratory at any time.
  3. Handle all equipment with care. Report any damage immediately.
  4. Log out of all accounts and properly shut down your PC before leaving.
  5. Arrange chairs and keep your workstation clean after each session.
  6. Unauthorized installation of software is strictly prohibited.
  7. Mobile phones must be on silent mode inside the laboratory.
  8. Students must log in via the Sit-in Monitoring System for every session.
  9. Maximum session duration is 2 hours per sit-in slot.
  10. Violators may have their lab privileges suspended or revoked.
